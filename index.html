⚓ A Tale of the Seven Seas…

Ahoy, ye scurvy dog! Listen well…

The Seven Seas be ruled by ignorance, and the ancient Islands of Knowledge lie scattered across the ocean — each one guardin' secrets of the legendary Copilot CLI.

Many a pirate has tried to conquer 'em all, but none had the wits nor the guts to answer the riddles that protect each shore. Get it right, and the island bows to yer flag. Get it wrong… well, ye'd best be handy with a cannon, because ghost ships be comin' fer yer hull!

Conquer all seven islands, prove yer knowledge be sharper than yer cutlass, and ye shall be crowned the one true Pirate King of the Knowledge Seas! ☠️👑
